\subsection{NAND生万物}
天地混沌，NAND为始。不知何人如何创建NAND门，然其功用至大，盖因其可构造任意逻辑电路也。

\begin{center}
\begin{circuitikz}
  \draw
  (0,0) node[nand port] (nand1) {\texttt{NAND}}
  (nand1.in 1) node[left] {$A$}
  (nand1.in 2) node[left] {$B$}
  (nand1.out) node[right] {$Y = \overline{A \cdot B}$};
\end{circuitikz}
\end{center}

\subsection{NOT逆阴阳}

  \draw[shift={(8,0)}]
